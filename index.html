ABOUT

Leading Teams & Products That Count is an invitation-only event for product leaders.

Some of the most accomplished executives in product, design and technology come together to collaborate, learn, and be inspired. Applicants are C-level executives from leading companies including Amazon, Mercedes-Benz, Hilton, Slack, Yelp, Asana, Facebook, Netflix and more.

Leading Teams & Products That Count is produced by Products That Count, a community of 12,000+ product managers and innovators.
