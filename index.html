ABOUT

Leading Teams & Products That Count is an invitation-only event for product leaders.

Some of the most accomplished executives in product, design and technology come together to collaborate, learn, and be inspired. The list of past participants includes leaders from Amazon, Facebook, Netflix, Tinder, Yelp and other hot companies.

Leading Teams & Products That Count is produced by Products That Count, one of the largest product communities.
